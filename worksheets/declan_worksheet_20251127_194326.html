declan’s Adventure Worksheet

Instructions

Read each question together. Point to the icons, count aloud, and use fingers or blocks. Take short movement breaks.

Part A: Counting & Matching (creatures & toys)

  • Count the friendly creatures: 3 then 2 more join. How many in all?
  • Circle the group with more: 4 toys vs 7 toys.
  • Match the number to pictures: 5 balloons, 3 stars, 6 blocks.
  • Point and count: How many rockets do you see? (pretend there are 8)

Part B: Simple Add/Subtract

  • A creature has 2 apples. A friend gives 3 more. How many apples?
  • There are 6 balloons. 2 float away. How many remain?
  • A toy box has 7 blocks. Declan builds with 4. How many are left?
  • Add the dots you imagine: 3 + 3 = ?

Part C: Fun Word Play

  • Clap once for each star when counting 1 to 5. How many claps in total?
  • Find the bigger number: 2 or 9?
  • If you see 4 cars and 1 rocket, how many vehicles?
  • Pretend you caught 3 creatures and then 2 more. How many now?

Parent tips

Celebrate effort and small wins. Use visuals, gentle pacing, and positive reinforcement. Keep sessions short and fun.
